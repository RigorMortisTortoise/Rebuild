What is the Rebuild Challenge?

The Copart Rebuild Challenge is a photo contest where Members can submit photos of their rebuilds for a chance to win cash prizes. This year, we’re opening the contest to Copart Basic and Premier Members in the U.S., Canada and Mexico.
